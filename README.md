## The Problem

Choose-your-own-adventure games are fun, but they don't tell you anything about *yourself*. What do your choices say about who you are?

## The Solution

AdventureMaker generates branching narratives with AI, tracks every decision you make, and delivers a personalized psychological assessment at the end. Are you a risk-taker or a strategist? Compassionate or pragmatic? Play to find out.

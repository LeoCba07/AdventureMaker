## üéÆ About

AdventureMaker is more than a choose-your-own-adventure game. As you navigate through AI-generated stories, your choices are tracked and analyzed. At the end of your journey, you receive a personalized psychological assessment based on the decisions you made. Are you a risk-taker or a strategist? Compassionate or pragmatic? Play to find out.

**Live Demo:** [Adventure Maker](https://adventuremaker-shinowfu-91409f739c06.herokuapp.com/)

## ‚ú® Features

### AI-Powered Storytelling
- **Dynamic narratives** ‚Äî Generates unique story content based on your choices
- **Branching paths** ‚Äî Every decision leads to different outcomes
- **Contextual continuity** ‚Äî The AI remembers your previous choices and weaves them into the story

### AI Image Generation
- **Scene illustrations** ‚Äî Google Gemini creates images that match your story moments
- **Visual immersion** ‚Äî See your adventure come to life with generated artwork

### Psychological Assessment
- **Choice tracking** ‚Äî Every decision you make is recorded throughout your adventure
- **AI-powered analysis** ‚Äî At journey's end, it analyzes your decision patterns
- **Personality insights** ‚Äî Receive a personalized assessment revealing your tendencies and traits
- **Self-discovery** ‚Äî Learn something new about yourself through play

### User Experience
- **Simple interface** ‚Äî Focus on the story, not complicated menus
- **Save your adventures** ‚Äî Return to your stories anytime
- **Share results** ‚Äî Compare your psychological profile with friends
